The Dominion · San Antonio, TX

Limestone & Steel
Contemporary Estate

5Bedrooms
6Bathrooms
7,200Square Feet
1.2Acres

Situated behind the gates of The Dominion on one of the most private parcels in the development, this limestone and steel residence represents a rare convergence of architectural precision and Hill Country materiality. Designed by a nationally recognized firm, the home was conceived as a response to both the Texas light and the natural topography of the site.

The main living areas open entirely to a curated outdoor environment — covered terraces, negative edge pool, and a deep summer kitchen that disappears into the canopy of mature live oaks. The interiors maintain the same restraint: wide-plank white oak, integrated custom millwork, and hand-selected stone throughout.

  • Negative edge pool with spa and outdoor kitchen
  • Chef's kitchen with professional-grade appliances
  • Private primary suite wing with spa bath
  • 4-car motor court and finished garage
  • Smart home integration and Lutron lighting
  • Within The Dominion gated community
