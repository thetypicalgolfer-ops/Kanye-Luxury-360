The Story Behind the Practice

A Career Shaped
by Leadership

Kanye Concierge 360 was not born from a career in real estate — it was born from a career in high-stakes corporate leadership. After more than 15 years with Delta Air Lines — where he managed corporate portfolios exceeding $340M, led complex negotiations, and consistently delivered top-tier performance — he made a deliberate decision to bring that same discipline, strategic thinking, and client-first mentality to the world of luxury real estate.

That same commitment to excellence is reflected in his academic foundation, holding an MBA in Business Marketing along with a Bachelor's degree in Marketing, graduating Cum Laude, with additional focus in International Business and German—bringing both analytical depth and a global perspective to his work.

Today, that same mindset defines Kanye Concierge 360.

This is not a high-volume brokerage. It is a private advisory practice, selectively serving buyers, sellers, and landowners across the Greater San Antonio and Texas Hill Country markets. Every client relationship is approached with intention, backed by data-driven insight, and guided by a clear understanding of both market dynamics and individual goals.

Known for a calm, consultative style, he brings clarity to complex transactions and confidence to critical decisions — whether guiding a first-time buyer through their first purchase or advising on a significant property investment. His global perspective, business acumen, and commitment to excellence create an experience that is both highly personal and results-driven. What he may lack in years behind a real estate desk, he more than makes up for in the boardroom-tested skills that matter most: negotiation, strategy, and an unwavering commitment to his clients' outcomes.

"At Kanye Concierge 360, every engagement is treated as if it is the only one on the desk—because for our clients, it is."
